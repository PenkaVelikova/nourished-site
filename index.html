The philosophy

This is not about fixing what is broken.
It is about understanding what flourishing looks like for you.

01
Not fixing. Flourishing.

Most approaches to wellbeing begin with what is wrong. This one begins with what is possible. Rooted in Positive Psychology and Humanistic Counselling, the work here is not about diagnosing or repairing — it is about understanding what a genuinely good life looks and feels like for you, and moving toward it.

02
The person, not the problem

Humanistic psychology holds that every person is inherently capable of growth, meaning-making, and self-understanding — given the right conditions. You are not broken. You may simply be undernourished. That is a different starting point, and it leads somewhere different.

03
The severed thread

Modern life has quietly dismantled the ancient relationship between food, meaning, and belonging. We have kept the eating. We have lost the nourishment. Restoring that thread — personally, deliberately — is one of the most radical acts of self-care available to us.

The app

Meet Nourished

A conversational companion rooted in Positive Psychology and Humanistic Counselling. Not a therapy app. Not a wellness tracker. A space that begins not from what is wrong — but from what is possible for you.

Using food, cooking, and the philosophy of nourishment as a doorway into deeper questions about meaning, belonging, and what genuinely flourishing looks like in your life.

For those already in therapy, Nourished is the daily space between sessions.

Nourished is not a replacement for therapy or medical support. If you are in crisis or need clinical help, please speak with a qualified professional or your GP.
